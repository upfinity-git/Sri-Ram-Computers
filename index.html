yes, we offer pickup, delivery, and onsite service depending on the requirement and location.

Yes, we offer refurbished systems at affordable prices. All systems are fully tested and in good working condition. You can choose from various configurations based on your needs — ideal for students, offices, and home use.

Our AMC covers routine checkups, software support, hardware diagnostics, cleaning and priority service.
